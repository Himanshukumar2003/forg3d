About Forg3d

Who we are?

Formed in 2001, as a small scale enterprise which was only making MS iron Railings, we have evolved to be a leader in the entire range of Wrought Iron Components.

Forg3d is a Delhi based wrought iron manufacturing company which specializes in delivering high end 3D Sheet Metals products, MS Railings, Forging Railheads & Balusters and other ornaments for gates and grills.

In addition, our forging unit in Samalkha (Distt. Panipat) comprise of multiple hammers to deliver high quality forging railheads and balusters and that too on committed time.

The key positions, been held by Vipin Aggarwal who is the backbone of the company who handles the entire production unit and Puneet Aggarwal is the face of the company.

Over the years it has been proven that quality of our products has been our prime selling point and we ensure giving high quality products at the best price possible in the market.
